# Quick Start

Get your first Arbital bot running in under 10 minutes.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Before you begin:** Please review our [Risk Disclosure](../legal/risk-disclosure.md) to understand the risks associated with automated trading.
{% endhint %}

## Prerequisites

- A Web3 wallet (MetaMask for EVM, Phantom for Solana)
- Funds on a supported exchange ($20+ for Pacifica/Extended)
- An invite code (beta access required)

## Step 1: Get Access

Arbital is currently in beta. To get started:

**Option A: Invite Code**
1. Get an invite code from an existing user or the team
2. Visit [arbital.xyz](https://arbital.xyz)
3. Connect your wallet
4. Enter your invite code when prompted
5. Access granted

**Option B: Join Waitlist**
1. Visit [arbital.xyz](https://arbital.xyz)
2. Click **Join Waitlist**
3. Enter your Telegram ID
4. You'll be notified when access is granted

## Step 2: Connect Your Wallet

1. Click **Connect Wallet**
2. Choose your wallet type:
   - **EVM wallets** (MetaMask, Rainbow) for Lighter & Extended
   - **Solana wallets** (Phantom, Solflare) for Pacifica
3. Sign the authentication message
4. You're connected

## Step 3: Create a Bot

1. Click **Create Bot** from the dashboard
2. **Select Strategy:** [Neutral](../reference/glossary.md#delta-neutral) (recommended for beginners)
3. **Select Exchange:** [Lighter](../reference/glossary.md#lighter), [Pacifica](../reference/glossary.md#pacifica), or [Extended](../reference/glossary.md#extended)
4. **Connect Wallet:** Sign to [bind credentials](../reference/glossary.md#credential-binding)
5. **Select Asset:** ETH, BTC, or other available pairs
6. **Set [Budget](../reference/glossary.md#budget):** How much to allocate (default: 30% of balance)
7. **Choose [Mode](../reference/glossary.md#execution-mode):** Aggressive, Normal, or Passive
8. Click **Create Bot**

Your bot automatically starts after creation.

## Step 4: Monitor

From the dashboard, track:

- **Balance Chart** — Your account balance over time
- **Volume Chart** — Trading activity
- **Progress Bars** — Volume vs target, fees vs budget
- **Events Table** — Order placements, fills, errors
- **Positions** — Current open positions

## Bot Limits

- Maximum **2 active bots** per account
- Minimum $20 balance for Pacifica and Extended
